Asus Zenbook S16 (Review)

Check Price & Availability

The Asus Zenbook S16 is super powerful for people who need a portable laptop that can handle a ton of software. Its Ryzen AI 9 processor with 4 Performace and 8 Efficiency cores is super reliable and efficient.

It is known for being super light for its power and the finish of the body is super sleek, creating no fingerprints. The Display is one of the best features with it being 120hz and 3K OLED.

Ports: 1x HDMI, 2x USB4 Type-C, Audio Jack, 1x Full SD Card Reader, 1x USB Type-A

Key Specifications

Display 16" 3K 120Hz OLED
CPU AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 370
RAM 32GB LPDDR5
GPU Dedicated Intel Arc
Storage 1TB NVMe SSD
Materials Ceraluminum Lid
Weight 1.5 kg (3.3 lbs)
Thickness 1.1 cm (0.43 inches)
Color Black or White

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Stunning OLED display
  • Incredibly thin and light
  • Premium build materials
  • Excellent speakers

Cons

  • Known to get warm under load
  • Integrated graphics gets behind for gaming

Final Verdict

This laptop is probably one of the better "built" laptops. For the price, it is a good-value device that can handle a ton. Having thermal issues is a strong con, thats why i'm not putting it higher.
